Shree Ram Janambhumi

Shree Ram Janambhumi _Shree Ram ji ka janamsthan hai jo hinduo k anusar hai.Shree Ram ji Vishnu Bhagwan k satve avatar mane jate hai.yeh whi janam sthan hai jiska vistar Ramayan mai iss prakar hai ki..saryu nadi k kinare ,ek shehar ayodhya hai jha p Shree Ram ji ki janambhumi h.. Aaj k samay mai ayodhya UttarPradesh mai sthit hai.Jaha par phla musalman majhab ki babri masjid thi, Thik usi sthan par Shree Ram janambhumi hai.1992 mai hinduo dwara babri masjid mai toda fodi ki gyi ,jis se puri babri masjid tehas nehas ho gyi.jiski wajah se yeh mudda supreme court chla gya.Bhut saal bit gya pr ispr koi faisla na aaya, us samay congress party ki sarkar satta mai thi.Jb BJP ki satta ai tb is vivad pr dobara sunvai hui.Jo august se october 2019 tak supreme court mai vivad mai rahi.Congress ne 24 vakil lgae Shree Ram mandir k khilaf ki mandir na bane .Pr satya ki jeet hui, Sanatan ki jit hui ,Hinduo ki jeet hui.Supreme court mai jis mahan itihasik vyakti RambhadraCharyaJi ne praman dia ki 1518s mai lagbhag 75000 hinduo ko markar Shree Ram janambhumi mandir tod ke vha p babri masjid bnai gyi jiska nirman mir banki ne jo ki mugal samrat tha usna 1528-29 tk kiya. Ramayan mai rachit sare kathan dikhae jo sidh kr rha the ki Shree Ram janambhumi vahi sthit hai jha babri masjid thi aur in sbhi sabato se 9 november 2019 mai faisla aaya ki wo bhumi Shree Ram ji ki hi janambhumi hai aur waha pr awedh babri masjid tod kar Shree Ram Mandir bnaya jae.Jo Shree Ram ji 500 saal tk tambu mai viraje, unha 500 saal baad apna is banwas se nyaye mila aur ab apni janam sthan_janam bhumi_ayodhya me viraje..
